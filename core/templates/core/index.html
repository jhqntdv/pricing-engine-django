02. OPTIONS DOCUMENTATION

MODEL: BLACK-SCHOLES / LOCAL VOLATILITY
DESCRIPTION: Standard European and American options pricing engine.

The options calculator computes NPV and standard Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Vega). Ensure the underlying ticker is valid and the spot price reflects current market conditions.

03. EXOTICS DOCUMENTATION

MODEL: MONTE CARLO SIMULATION
DESCRIPTION: Pricing for path-dependent and barrier options.

The exotics engine relies on the SSVI volatility surface for accurate barrier touch probabilities. Computations may take slightly longer due to the required simulation paths.

04. EQUITY LINKED NOTES (ELNs) DOCUMENTATION

MODEL: HYBRID FIXED INCOME / EQUITY
DESCRIPTION: Structured product issuance pricing.

Decomposes the ELN into a zero-coupon bond and an embedded derivative (typically a call option or basket option). Yields the fair issue price and component values.
